# Scorecard

This file defines the grading rubric for future Codex, Cursor, Claude, Pi, or
generic-agent runs. It is not itself an agent output.

| Criterion | Weight | Pass evidence | Deduction trigger |
|---|---:|---|---|
| Style authority and product context | 15 | Checks brand/style authority and the landing page's product promise before recommending polish. | Replaces the page with a stock SaaS landing template. |
| Reference selection | 15 | Routes to visual judgment, foundational principles, design moves, product taste, and validation references as needed. | Ignores product UI taste guidance or reads unrelated references. |
| Anti-generic redesign | 15 | Identifies vague hero copy, equal feature cards, generic gradients, or missing proof sequence. | Adds decorative effects without improving product specificity. |
| Evidence level labeling | 15 | Labels whether the input is static, browser-backed, responsive, or before/after. | Assigns a high score without evidence level or anti-inflation limits. |
| Verified/unverified boundary | 15 | Separates visible hierarchy/copy issues from unverified conversion, interaction, and mobile behavior. | Claims conversion, performance, or mobile polish without proof. |
| Concrete design moves | 15 | Recommends product-specific claim, proof-first hero, varied section rhythm, and concrete CTA hierarchy. | Gives vague "make it premium" advice without implementable moves. |
| Scope control and unrelated changes | 10 | Keeps recommendations scoped to landing UI, copy hierarchy, and validation evidence. | Suggests unrelated architecture or backend changes. |
